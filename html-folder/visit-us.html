Find Us

Location & Directions

SLC Art Gallery
1452 S West Temple St
Salt Lake City, UT 84115

Located in the heart of Salt Lake City's arts district, just south of downtown. Easily accessible by public transit and within walking distance of local restaurants and shops.

Getting Here

  • By Car: Free street parking available. Paid lot behind building.
  • By Bus: UTA Routes 9, 21, and 217 stop within one block.
  • By TRAX: Central Pointe Station is a 10-minute walk.

Accessibility

SLC Art is committed to making our gallery accessible to all visitors. Our facility is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms. Wheelchairs are available for loan at the front desk. Service animals are welcome. For specific accommodation requests, please contact us in advance.
