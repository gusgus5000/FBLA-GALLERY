Our Artists

SLC Art showcases work from talented artists across Utah and the Mountain West region. Each artist brings a unique perspective and style to our gallery.

Maya Chen

Mixed Media & Sculpture

Maya Chen explores themes of identity and place through layered collages and sculptural installations. Her work has been featured in galleries across the Mountain West region. Drawing inspiration from her multicultural background, Maya creates pieces that bridge traditional techniques with contemporary expression.

James Rodriguez

Documentary Photography

A documentary photographer capturing the changing landscape of Salt Lake City, James's work examines urban growth and the intersection of nature and development. His photographs tell stories of community transformation and the resilience of local neighborhoods.

Sarah Kim

Abstract Painting

Sarah's vibrant abstract paintings draw inspiration from the colors and textures of Utah's desert landscapes. She uses bold brushwork to create emotionally resonant compositions that capture the essence of the American Southwest and the changing seasons of the Great Basin.

Marcus Thompson

Light Photography

Marcus specializes in long-exposure light photography that captures the hidden energy of the spectrum. His innovative techniques reveal the beauty in motion and light, creating otherworldly images that challenge viewers to see the world in new ways.

Elena Vasquez

Watercolor & Mixed Media

Elena's watercolor works capture the ethereal beauty of Utah's natural landscapes. Her mixed media pieces blend traditional watercolor techniques with contemporary materials, creating dreamlike compositions that evoke the serenity of the Great Salt Lake region.

David Park

Abstract Textures

David's monochromatic abstract works explore the raw beauty of natural transitions. His textured pieces evoke the powerful shift of seasons, capturing moments of melting ice and emerging earth in stunning visual compositions that speak to the cyclical nature of time.

Become a Featured Artist

SLC Art is always seeking talented artists from Utah and the Mountain West region. If you're interested in showcasing your work at our gallery, we'd love to hear from you.

Submission Guidelines

  • Portfolio of 10-15 recent works
  • Artist statement and biography
  • High-resolution images of your pieces
  • Contact information and social media links

Email submissions to: artists@slcart.org
