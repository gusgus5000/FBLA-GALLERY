About SLC Art Gallery

SLC Art is Salt Lake City's premier destination for contemporary art, showcasing the vibrant creativity of local and regional artists in an intimate, accessible gallery space.

Our misson

We believe art has the power to inspire, challenge, and unite communities. Through carefully curated exhibitions and educational programs, SLC Art connects artists with audiences and fosters a deeper appreciation for contemporary visual culture.

Visit Us

Whether you're an art enthusiast or just curious, everyone is welcome at SLC Art. Explore our current exhibitions, attend an artist talk, or simply enjoy the creative atmosphere.

What Makes Us Special

  • Focus on Utah and Mountain West artists
  • Rotating exhibitions every 6-8 weeks
  • Free admission for students and children
  • Artist talks and community events
  • Accessible downtown location

Maya Chen

Grammy-nominated violinist whose paintings capture the emotional intensity of her performances. Maya's journey began in classical conservatories, but her artistic vision extends far beyond the concert hall. Each brushstroke echoes the passion she brings to her violin, creating a unique visual symphony that resonates with collectors worldwide.
